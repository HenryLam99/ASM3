Project Description

Overview

When assignment 3 started our team had a lot of cool ideas for the project and we also considered if the project idea from assignment 2 was realistic and would help society, have a huge impact on the IT industry or not. After giving out a lot of ideas we decided to do the “Hand Glove for Physical Challenged people (deaf/mute)”. We all love studying about new programing languages and learn new things about IT but one thing that was not consider ways to use our knowledge and technology to help physically challenged people and also this is an opportunity for use to learn about something that does not often come up in the course, limit testing ourself on something extremely new. The IT industry trend in 2021 is a lot about helping businesses to secure their customer data or better customer service and communication, or creating new content for people to follow on social media, cloud computing but helping people that was not so lucky and have physical disabilities was not a trend and people started to forget that we create new technology is to make our life become better. So even though we are really familiar with coding and other software base projects we decided to try this project idea. This is something that does not help our team members' ideal job as we all want to have a job that is mostly about developing software but helping the society better is something that is really valuable to learn and may help us in the future. Even though the comment on our project idea was a really good idea and quite complex to do but it not something quite new and challenging also we thought that people put so much effort into the entertainment industry and use technology to make it even better but we had forgot to help people with disabilities to have a better life so that they may not feel like they have a disadvantage in life compared to normal people.

Detailed Description

Aims

Our aims for this project is to help change the way that deaf and mute people communicate with normal people, after lot of research about problems that deaf and mute people may met in their daily life we found out that even with the existing technology like the bone conduction headphone that help the deaf people can hear what other people is saying but the problem is that when they use the “hand-talk” languages to communicate with normal people a lot of people do not understand that languages and so that they will not understand what the deaf and mute people so that with our glove we aims that a cheap and familiar to user products so that help disabilities people to have a easier life to communicate to other people. Nowaday people started to focus more on the entertainment industry and using the technology to enhance it but people with less luck and with disabilities did not have that same fun and have a lot of difficulties in their life, also helping disabilities people was never an IT industry trend so our team want to help the society and make people realise that using technology is about helping people are not lucky like us to have the same life experiences like everyone else.

Plans and Progress

When assignment 3 started we contacted each other and then set a day to start working on the project. When we schedule the meeting we first look back at the project idea from the past assignment and find out that it was quite complex and some parts are almost unrealistic or is really hard for us in order to do or have an idea that everyone will understand about the project. So we started to do research and gave out a lot of interesting ideas after voting and thinking if the project is possible for us to do we decided on the “hand glove for physical challenges(deaf/mue)”. This is based on a real life scenario of a deaf person in daily life and the project will help them to solve the problems.Until now, when we have had the opportunity to follow our desired career and learn about incredible things. That which appears to be far-fetched is actually within grasp, which is just what we are discovering. That was our inspiration for creating the product for our final ASM. A glove with an associated application that helps to adapt speech and signals to make it simpler may communicate speech through motions. With this device, we believe that persons with disabilities will have a better quality of life and will no longer feel excluded from society. At first the project idea sounded really cool and interesting. Also this project is something that was not similar to all of us as this requires our team members to have a strong knowledge about understanding how hardware works, especially Arduino. This project is a really challenging task for all of us but we decided to test our limits. First we started to do the research on what area of the project, for example how it works, will people decide to use it or not,... after finding out areas that needed to be solved we started to fit in our role to solve the problems. We use the google Doc to write down the script and share it with other team members after a few days of working on the script and fixing what is wrong, we decided to do the presentation as Nhat he decided to use a famous film that mainly talks about life of a deaf girl and how her problems impacts her in order for other students can easily understand why we do project and what our project will help to solve all those problems. This was asked and agreed by all team members. When we do the power points we do it together and each team member gives out their idea of what they saw wrong on their slide and gives out an idea to solve the problem. After a few days of editing and fixing the slides we finally had a complete version of the powerpoint presentation. Then our team members schedule a meeting so that we can practice a quick talk to prepare mentally and physically for the presentation day. We gave feedback on how each team member can improve their presenting skills in front of the class and have a strong mentality so that we will not make mistakes when the presentation day comes. Even though some members struggled when standing in front of the whole class and they could not perform 100% as practice, we still did a good job on our roles. We had talked about our research data show and about how the device works,.. This task was quite hard for our team members as we did not have much skill on hardware and we are not familiar with standing in front of the audience. But after the presentation day we gave out a clear aim that people should use technology to help people who were not lucky to have normal life like us to enhance their life experience instead of just focusing on entertaining our life and forgetting the people that were not so lucky. We had created a prototype for the project we planned to bring it in front of the whole class but sadly the project did not work like we planned. Maybe it was because our skill was not good enough to make it worked properly. So that is why we only had one slide about the picture of the project. After the presentation day we sat down with each other to talk about what we could have done better and to try to improve our mentality when presenting in front of the class.

Scope and Limits

Because from the beginning the project was aimed at a small market, people with disabilities. People make up only a small percentage of a large market, so our initial vision was not really high, simply serving a normal and easier life for people with disabilities. The project has only a few simple functions of converting sign language into speech and establishing its own sign language. The development potential of the project is great but not enough to hit a broader market. Right now we are only freshmen, so we still don't have much experience in product development. But with the vision we had, we were sure my product would be very successful after it came out but would falter at some point. What we need to do is improve it and maybe target more people than people with disabilities. We can turn it into a multi-functional glove such as a language interpreter, health check or add an IoT function to connect with other devices around, so everything will be easier because things are directly through the hands. According to our vision. This product will be completed in about sophomore year and it will probably be a potential project for us to use in our senior year once it is completed and developed.

Testing

We used the Arduino kit for this project and we tried to connect wires, main board and gloves together. It isn’t easy for the first time to make a project like this so we have to do some research on the internet for it. We used a simulator for this project because our team doesn't have enough equipment to make a completed product. We have to deal with a lot of issues like the gloves need an Android app to produce the sound so we tried our best to do it. But the code that we set up for the product works well and nothing is unusual. We tried to test on some disable person as a volunteer but we couldn't find anyone so we tested by ourselves. If we can find a volunteer, one or two people will be enough for testing the product.
